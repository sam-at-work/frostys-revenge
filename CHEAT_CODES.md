# Cheat Codes

## Boss Teleport

**Code:** Type `b` `o` `s` `s` (in that order)

**Effect:** Instantly teleports the player to the start of the Santa boss fight area.

**Details:**
- You must type the letters within 2 seconds of each other
- The sequence resets if you type a wrong letter
- When activated, the cheat will:
  - Teleport the player to the boss area (x: 4350)
  - Move the camera to the boss area
  - Start the snow particle effects
  - Switch to boss music
  - Allow you to immediately engage in the boss fight

**Usage:**
This is useful for testing the boss fight without having to play through the entire level each time.

## Notes
- Cheat codes work at any time during gameplay (except when dying/respawning)
- Multiple cheat codes may be added in the future
